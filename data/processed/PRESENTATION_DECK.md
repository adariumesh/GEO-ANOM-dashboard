## SLIDE 6: Revenue Model

**Header:** Diversified Revenue Streams

**Content:**

**Dual Revenue Sources:**

**1. Renewable Energy (50%): $19.1M/year**

## SLIDE 7: Economic Resilience

**Header:** Strong Performance Across All Scenarios

**Content:**

**Sensitivity Analysis Results:**

**Transport Costs:** $1.50 - $4.00/km
- NPV Range: $123M - $154M
- **Finding:** Profitable even at high fuel prices

**Energy Prices:** $0.08 - $0.18/kWh
- NPV Range: $74M - $238M
- **Finding:** Positive NPV in all price scenarios

**Digestate Prices:** $10 - $25/ton
- Revenue Impact: $25.6M - $60.8M/year
- **Finding:** Conservative base assumptions ($15/ton)

**Construction Costs:** $1.5M - $3.0M per hub
- NPV Range: $132M - $147M
- **Finding:** Tolerable to 50% overruns

**Visual:**
- Tornado diagram showing NPV sensitivity

**Header:** Phased Deployment: From Pilot to Full Network

**Content:**

**3-Phase Approach:**

**PHASE 1: Pilot (Year 1) - Worcester County**
- Deploy: 2-3 hubs
- Investment: $4-6M
- AFOs: 60-80 facilities
- Objective: Validate model
- **Outcome:** Proven operations, revenue confirmed

**PHASE 2: Expansion (Years 2-3) - Regional**
- Add: 4 hubs (Caroline, Wicomico, Somerset)
- Investment: $8M
- AFOs: 150-200 cumulative
- Objective: Scale operations

**PHASE 3: Full Network (Years 4-5)**

- Investment: $6M
